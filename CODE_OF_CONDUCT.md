# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the SARA Open community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient with new users and contributors

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Sharing malicious content or attempting to abuse SARA's capabilities
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to [support@sirp.io](mailto:support@sirp.io). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project team reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
